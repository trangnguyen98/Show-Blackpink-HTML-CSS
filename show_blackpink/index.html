THE SHOW

We love music

The Show is an online concert by the Korean girl group Blackpink after nearly a year of the group's almost complete absence from tours due to the impact of the global COVID-19 pandemic. This online concert was originally scheduled to take place on December 27, 2020 but due to the outbreak of the COVID-19 pandemic in Korea, it was moved to January 31, 2021.
